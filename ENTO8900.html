Advanced Quantitative Analysis

ENTO 8900--Fall 2022; Spring 2023

Syllabus

Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

1. Recognize different functions and syntax/language used in R.

2. Interpret textbook/online example code to ube used with your own data.

3. Complete varous simple statistical analyses useing various types of data.

4. Determine the appropriate analysis based on the type of data/experimental design.

5. Design and manipulate data visualizations of publication quality.

6. Effectively defend/explain data analyses and interpretation of results.
